🏠 About Ethiosadat Furniture

Quality furniture at affordable prices, serving Ethiopian homes with pride since 2020

📖 Our Story

Ethiosadat Furniture was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful vision: to make quality furniture accessible to every Ethiopian home. Starting from a small workshop in Addis Ababa, we have grown into a trusted name in the furniture industry, serving thousands of satisfied customers across the country.

Our journey has been driven by a passion for craftsmanship, attention to detail, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. Every piece of furniture we create is a blend of traditional Ethiopian aesthetics and modern design.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, stylish, and affordable furniture that transforms houses into homes, while delivering exceptional customer service and value.
